About Video Utilities

I made this because I needed a simple tool to compress videos for Discord without dealing with bad and slow software or websites since their performance is even worse and they often add subscriptions.
I then proceeded to add other cool features to show the kind of stuff you can make with ffmpeg (the trimming feature is my favorite). And now it became this!

It's just a local app that uses FFmpeg to do the heavy lifting. It can even choose the right encoding method for your GPU automatically (NVENC, AMF, VAAPI, etc.).

Is this vibe coded?

Yes, I know some people don't like vibe coded programs, but I am simply not that experienced with coding using Flet so I had to use AI.

Everything is powered by FFmpeg under the hood, the UI is built with Flet (Python) and tries to follow Material Design. There might be some bugs here and there but it gets the job done.
